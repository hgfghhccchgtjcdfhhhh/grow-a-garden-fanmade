Mutations and Weather: ⛈️

Weathers from worst 💩 to best! 😱

No Weather: Normal Growth 🌅
Rain: 50%+ Growth Speed, chances for crops to turn wet. 🌦️
Frost: 50%+ Growth Speed, chances for crops to turn chilled, wet or frozen. 🥶
Disco (Admin Only): Chances for crops to turn Disco and your avatar dances. 🪩
Thunderstorm: Small chances for crops to turn Shocked, no global area for crop mutating. ⛈️
Meteor Storm: Chances for crops to turn Celestial (best mutation). ☄️

Rainbow: 0.1%, use sprinklers to get chances higher, 50x (including 50x total chance). 🌈
Gold: 1% Chance, use sprinklers to get chances higher, 20x (including 20x total chance). ⚜️
Moonlit: Comes in Night Events/Weather, 2x (you can give it to the Wise Old Owl). 🌑
Bloodlit: Comes in Blood Moon Events/Weather 4x (gives more lunar points to the Wise Old Owl). 🩸

Trading: 📈

How to properly trade different stuff: 🧸 Lunar Points: Give your Moonlit/Bloodlit stuff to other people and they give you stuff in return (MAKE THEM ALWAYS GO FIRST)
Valueble Pets: Pets such as racoons, red foxes, blood owls, night owls, etc. are REALLY valueble pets (MAKE THEM ALWAYS GO FIRST)
Racoon Worth: 1 Trillion.
Dragon Fly Worth: 400 Billion.
Red Fox Worth: 200 Billion.
Blood Owl Worth: UNKNOWN.
Night Owl Worth: 15 Billion. 📈📈📈📈📈
